What We Offer
Penetration Testing

Penetration testing is fundamental to any robust cybersecurity strategy, aiming to identify vulnerabilities in systems and networks before they can be exploited by malicious actors. SeKurd Penetration Testing service enhances threat visibility by simulating attacks from external, internal, and rogue user perspectives. Utilizing the same Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures (TTPs) as real-world adversaries and Advanced Persistent Threat (APT) actors, our penetration tests offer a precise and efficient approach to adversary simulation. These tests are customized to specific scenarios and executed within a shorter timeframe, delivering a comprehensive and realistic assessment of your security posture. 

Learn more
Application Security

At SeKurd, our Application Security services are designed to protect your organization’s software from evolving threats and vulnerabilities. With the increasing complexity of applications and the growing sophistication of cyberattacks, securing your applications is more critical than ever. Our comprehensive approach ensures that your applications remain secure throughout their lifecycle, from development to deployment and beyond. 

Learn more
Red Team Operations

At Sekurd, our Red Team operations extend beyond standard penetration testing by conducting comprehensive simulations of real-world attacks across your entire organization. This approach allows you to evaluate your team's and security systems' effectiveness in responding to actual threats. Our red-teaming scope encompasses networks, web applications, routers, security appliances, and even employees. Unlike conventional penetration testing, our Red Team employs a range of tactics, including social engineering, network penetration, and web application testing, to thoroughly assess your organization's security posture.

Learn more
CyberSecurity Training

At SeKurd, we offer comprehensive security training programs designed to empower your team with the knowledge and skills needed to effectively manage and mitigate cybersecurity risks. Our training services are tailored to address the unique needs of your organization, ensuring that all employees—from technical staff to executives—understand and can implement best practices for safeguarding your information assets.

Vulnerability Management

At SeKurd, we provide comprehensive Vulnerability Management services designed to protect your organization from emerging threats and potential security risks. Compliance

SeKurd’s regulatory compliance services are designed to help clients identify vulnerabilities, assess risk impact, and implement effective remediation measures. We ensure that technology, processes, and personnel within your organization meet industry-specific or national standards such as PCI-DSS, ISO 27001, GDPR, PDPA, and HIPAA.
